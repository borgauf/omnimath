* Appendix A

Kathy had been advised by her Clojure programming colleague to use the [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Emacs][Emacs]] editor, which, he said, was what all the "big boys and girls" use. Once she had Emacs going, the parentheses forest seemed manageable: First, Emacs formatted the Scheme code with common sense indentation. Second, you could highlight any particular parenthesis---and its matching opposite partner would light up. She was also encouraged to learn and use [[https://git-scm.com/][git]] to "version control" everything she did. Steep learning curve, but she had anticipated a hard uphill slog. [See Appendix B]
