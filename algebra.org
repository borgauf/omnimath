** Al-Khw√§rizmi

Al-Khw√§rizmi is credited with inventing algebra. The word algebra
comes from /al-jabr/, which is Al-Khw√§rizmi's word---along with
/al-muqabala/---for adding, subtracting, in general, balancing terms
on both sides of an equation. His first equation was

\begin{align*}
x^2 = 40x - 4 x^2
\end{align*}

then using /al-jabr/ and /al-muqabala/

\begin{align*}
5x^2 &= 40x \\
x^2 &= 8x \\
x &= 8
\end{align*}
